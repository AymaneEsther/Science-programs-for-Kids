Chemistry

Chemistry helps students understand why certain things happen, and how differnt substances interact with each other. There is a reason why Chemistry is a required class in most highschools.

Chemistry programs available for Highschool kids in Philadelphia who may be falling behind.

Drexel University

Drexel University offers a range of programs for high school students, including a chemistry camp. The camp is designed to introduce students to the basics of chemistry through hands-on experiments and activities.

University of Pennsylvania

The University of Pennsylvania offers a summer program called Penn Summer High School Programs, which includes a chemistry course for high school students. The program is taught by experienced Penn faculty members and provides students with an opportunity to explore chemistry concepts in depth.

Science Leadership Academy

Science Leadership Academy: This public high school offers a rigorous science curriculum that includes chemistry as one of the core subjects. The school has state-of-the-art science labs and experienced teachers who help students develop a deep understanding of chemistry concepts.
